## ğŸ“Š FEATURE 1: TRADING SIGNALS (8-Factor Scoring System)

### WHAT IT IS:
Composite score (0-100) dari 8 faktor market data untuk generate LONG/SHORT/NEUTRAL signals dengan confidence levels.

### 8 FACTORS EXPLAINED:

**1. Liquidations (Weight: 25%)**
- Tracks longs vs shorts liquidated
- High long liq = bearish (retail trapped)
- High short liq = bullish (squeeze potential)

**2. Funding Rate (Weight: 18%)**
- Cost untuk hold perpetual positions
- Positive = longs pay shorts (overcrowded longs)
- Negative = shorts pay longs (bearish sentiment)

**3. Price Momentum (Weight: 20%)**
- Multi-timeframe trend analysis (5m to 24h)
- Confirms direction & strength
- Detects reversals early

**4. Long/Short Ratio (Weight: 12%)**
- Retail trader positioning
- >70% longs = overcrowded (contrarian bearish)
- >60% shorts = potential squeeze

**5. Smart Money (Weight: 12%)**
- Top trader positioning
- Whale accumulation/distribution
- Institutional bias

**6. OI Trend (Weight: 8%)**
- Open interest growth/decline
- Rising OI + price up = healthy uptrend
- Falling OI + price up = weak rally

**7. Social Sentiment (Weight: 8%)**
- LunarCrush social metrics
- High social + high funding = FOMO top
- Low social + accumulation = early stage

**8. Fear & Greed (Weight: 7%)**
- Market-wide sentiment index
- Extreme Fear (0-25) = buy opportunity
- Extreme Greed (75-100) = sell signal

### CONFIDENCE LEVELS:
- **High (70-100)**: All factors align, strong conviction
- **Medium (40-69)**: Mixed signals, proceed with caution
- **Low (0-39)**: Conflicting data, wait for clarity

### WHEN TO USE:
- âœ… Quick market sentiment check
- âœ… Entry/exit decision validation
- âœ… Risk assessment before position
- âœ… Daily trading routine

### BEST PRACTICES:
1. Don't trade on score alone - verify with other features
2. High confidence signals more reliable
3. Contrarian approach often works (buy fear, sell greed)
4. Combine with technical analysis for timing
