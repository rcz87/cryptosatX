## ğŸ“‰ WHAT IS ATR (Average True Range)?

ATR mengukur volatility dengan melihat average price range over 14 periods.

**Formula:** ATR = average of (High - Low) over 14 candles

**Example:**
- BTC price: $43,000
- ATR: $1,200 (14-period)
- ATR %: 2.79% ($1,200 / $43,000)

**ATR < 1% (Very Low Volatility):**

- Low risk environment
- **Position Sizing:** 1.5x-2.0x (aggressive)

- Calm market, small moves
- Below average risk
- **Position Sizing:** 1.0x-1.25x

- Standard crypto volatility
- Average risk environment
- **Position Sizing:** 0.75x-1.0x (standard)
- **Stop Loss:** 3-5% from entry

**ATR 4-8% (High Volatility):**
- Large price swings
- Elevated risk
- **Position Sizing:** 0.5x-0.75x (reduced)
- **Stop Loss:** 5-10% from entry (wider)

**ATR > 8% (Very High Volatility):**
- Extreme price movements
- Very high risk
- **Position Sizing:** 0.25x-0.5x (minimal)
- **Stop Loss:** 10-15% from entry (very wide)
